Certifications

C)PTE - Certified Penetration Testing Engineer From Mile2 USA

A Penetration Testing Engineer is a cybersecurity expert who simulates cyberattacks to identify and exploit vulnerabilities in networks, systems, and applications. Their primary goal is to uncover weaknesses before malicious hackers do, helping organizations strengthen their security. They perform vulnerability assessments, exploit flaws, collaborate with IT teams on solutions, and provide detailed reports with remediation recommendations. Given the evolving nature of cyber threats, they must constantly update their skills and tools. Many pursue certifications like OSCP or CEH to validate their expertise, and they often work across industries like finance, healthcare, and government, with strong demand and career growth prospects

C)CSA - Certified Cybersecurity Analyst From Mile2 USA

The Check Point Certified Security Administrator (CCSA) is a professional certification that focuses on managing and securing network environments using Check Point’s solutions. It is designed for IT professionals involved in the deployment, configuration, and administration of Check Point technologies. This certification teaches key skills like installing security gateways, creating security policies, managing VPNs, monitoring traffic, and configuring clustering for high availability. Administrators learn to work with tools such as SmartConsole for policy management and troubleshoot security incidents effectively. Completing the CCSA helps professionals enhance their organization’s security posture and build expertise in network security solutions
